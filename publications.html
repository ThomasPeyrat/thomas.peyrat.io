Multivariate Self-Exciting Processes with Dependencies
Caroline Hillairet, Thomas Peyrat and Anthony Réveillac — 2025.
This paper introduces the class of multidimensional self-exciting processes with dependencies (MSPD), which is a unifying writing for a large class of processes: counting, loss, intensity, and also shifted processes. The framework takes into account dynamic dependencies between the frequency and the severity components of the risk, and therefore induces theoretical challenges in the computations of risk valuations.
