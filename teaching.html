Teaching

Probability, statistics, and project-based learning

PHILOSOPHY

I design my teaching around three principles: connecting rigorous theory to concrete problems, using projects to foster ownership and creativity, and building inclusive learning environments where students with diverse backgrounds can engage with quantitative tools.

EXPERIENCE

Introduction to mathematical finance
ENSAE — Undergraduate
Tutorials and problem-solving sessions in financial mathematics, emphasizing intuition and rigorous reasoning. Particular attention is given to connecting mathematical tools (stochastic processes, hedging, risk-neutral measure) with their real-world interpretations.
Project supervision
Applied mathematics and actuarial projects
Supervision of student projects on risk modeling and data analysis, including guidance on modeling choices, implementation, and presentation. Some of these projects are carried out within the framework required to obtain the French actuarial credential delivered by the Institut des Actuaires.
